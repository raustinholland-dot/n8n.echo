# DDA_OutputPatterns.md
Version: 1.0
Purpose: Authoritative formatting + structure rules for all DDA outputs.

---

## 0) Global Formatting Standard (MANDATORY)
- System font
- Clear section headers
- Prefer bullets over prose
- Paragraphs <= 3 lines
- Avoid dense blocks

---

## 1) Length & Constraint Rules (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
- Risks: <= 150 characters total
- Next Steps: <= 150 characters total
- Client follow-ups: <= 150 words
- Agendas: <= 5 bullets, no time allotments
- Follow-up correspondence assumes appropriate attachments are included

---

## 2) Salesforce Notes Patterns (examples + guardrails)
### 2.1 Deal Highlights / DAP timeline (pattern)
- Short, factual bullets
- Outcome-oriented
- Separate known vs unknown

Avoid:
- Adjectives (“great”, “exciting”)
- Speculation/optimism
- Long narrative

### 2.2 Current State / Desired State (pattern)
Use 3 lenses:
- ORGANIZATION
- ACTIVITIES
- TECHNOLOGY

### 2.3 Stakeholder Success Criteria (pattern)
- Board / Exec outcomes
- Executive Sponsor / Champion outcomes
- Use role labels where known

---

## 3) Risks + Next Steps (strict formats)

### 3.1 Risks (<=150 chars total)
- Single-line bullets only
- Must be concrete (budget, timeline, access, approvals, sequencing violations)

### 3.2 Next Steps (<=150 chars total)
- Format: `M/D: <INITIALS> - <action>`
- Single-line bullets only

---

## 4) Client Follow-Up Email Patterns

### 4.1 Discovery Follow-Up (<=150 words)
- Max 3 short paragraphs
- Thank them + summarize what drives progress
- Reference attachments
- Clear next steps with ownership

### 4.2 Post-Approach / Next Steps
- Thank them + confirm what changed / what’s attached
- Confirm next steps and timing
- No fluff, no overpromising

---

## 5) Internal Teams Message Pattern
- Bulleted
- Action-oriented
- Clear owner
- Attach / link referenced artifacts

---

## 6) Pre-Call Planner (Canonical)
Headers + bullets only:
- Client participants
- Internal participants + roles
- Customer website
- SFDC references (if available)
- About customer
- Purpose
- Desired outcome
- Current state
- Desired state
- Key discussion points + confirmatory questions
- Buying process + timeline questions

---

## 7) Call Agenda (Standard)
- Bulleted only
- Max 5 bullets
- No time allocations

---

## 8) Internal Team Update (Standard)
- Bulleted
- Factual
- No speculation

---

## 9) Internal Strategy Meeting (RARE)
Propose only when deal size/complexity/risk justifies it.
Include:
- Meeting title
- Purpose
- Context (deal facts only)
- Agenda
- Action items
